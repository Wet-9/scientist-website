Issac Newton


Issac Newton was born in 1643 and was a well known mathmatician, physicist, and astronomer.

His notable contribution include:

• Laws Of Motion
• Universal Gravitation and
• He was the founding father of Calculus.

There's a story of how gravity was discovered because Issac was sitting under a tree and an apple fell on his head.
An interesting fact about Issac Newton was that he was knighted by Queen Anne in 1705, becoming Sir Issac Newton!
